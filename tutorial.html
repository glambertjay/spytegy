Game Tutorial

Learn and Play in 5 Minutes

Tutorial Video (W/ explaination)

Game Summary

  • 2 players.
  • Player can choose any color, then the opponent would be the opposite one.
  • The Player can only see health points of his own pieces and both bases.
  • Player cannot see what the opponent's piece is.
  • Once a player attacks the opponent’s piece or is attacked by the opponent's Sapper, he must turn his piece to face up and show its identity. To hide the identity, that piece must go home

Set Up

  • Players place their own pieces at the starting point ( base ), the pieces should face down. Players can check their own pieces at any time.
  • Each pieces and base has max hit points

How to move your pieces

  • First specify a piece that you want to move.
  • Decide how many steps you want to move and move the piece.
  • Attack the opponent's pieces or stay where it is.

Combat rule

  • Roll the dice(s)
  • The success of the attack is determined by the outcome of the dice(s) roll
    • General: Toss 2 dice, if the sum of the two dice greater or equal to 5, general can deal 2 damage to the enemy.
    • Colonel: Toss 2 dice, if the sum of the two dice greater or equal to 8, colonel can deal 1 damage to enemy.
    • Captain: Toss 2 dice, if the sum of the two dice greater or equal to 7, captain can deal one damage to enemy
    • Sapper: Toss 2 dice, if one of them has 6 show up, the sapper can deal one damage. Sapper can force the attacked piece to face up, no matter whether the sapper deals the damage or not. The successful rate of Sapper attacking opponent’s base depends on dice points

How to win

  • Player can win the game, if he achieve one of the following statement
    • Eliminate all of the opponent’s pieces
    • Occupy the opponent's base by placing the piece into the opponent's base.
