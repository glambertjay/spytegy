Spytegy


Multiplayer Espionage Board Game

Play as an Espionage

Spytegy is a war strategy board game that simulates espionage in a war. This game adapted ideas from board game “Jungle”, “Stratego”, and “Ludo” but created with our origin map and rules. In Spytegy, each of two players will control their pieces by rolling the dice and placing the General piece to the opponent's base camp for a win.

Chess Pieces

  • General: Slow to move, small attack range, but has the highest attack power. moving range(0-3), attack range(1), attack value(2)

  • Colonel: moving range(0-4), attack range(3), attack value(1)

  • Captain: moving range(0-5), attack range(2), attack value(1)

  • Sapper: Fast movement, low attack range, low damage, but can know the opponent’s identity after attacking. moving range(0-6), attack range(1), attack value(1)
